Our Story

Elevating Events,

The Food Cartel brings the ultimate food and dessert experience to your events. From weddings and galas to corporate gatherings and private parties, our customizable stations let your guests build their own indulgent creations.

Based in Detroit, Michigan, we specialize in creating interactive food and dessert stations that become the highlight of any event. Every station is fully customizable — your guests choose their base, toppings, drizzles, and more to create something uniquely theirs.

We're a fully licensed LLC and insured, so you can trust us to deliver a professional, seamless experience every time.
